### Special Question: Bracket

Handle what signal open bracket "(" and close bracket ")" mean. Think about it like stack thread in PC where opening the bracket means saving history and starting new thread. Must be able to recover state once current stack thread closes.
Actions
* Bracket open: clean the currents
* Bracket close: pop from stack, update current from these
* What to store in stack: prev chars + prev nums, since this will allow reconstructing back where thread was left off (old chars + old nums * current chars).

[Decode String](https://leetcode.com/problems/decode-string/description/)
[Reverse Substring between Brackets](https://leetcode.com/problems/reverse-substrings-between-each-pair-of-parentheses/)
