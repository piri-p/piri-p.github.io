### Think about what to store in stack

For example,

* Waitlist of items that don't have the answer yet ([1475. Final Prices With a Special Discount in a Shop](https://leetcode.com/problems/final-prices-with-a-special-discount-in-a-shop/description/), [739. Daily Temperatures](https://leetcode.com/problems/daily-temperatures/description/))
* Next candidate like in 132 pattern

### Think about whether we need just the value, or also index to be able to backfill, or also entire history

* Index as well ([1475. Final Prices With a Special Discount in a Shop](https://leetcode.com/problems/final-prices-with-a-special-discount-in-a-shop/description/), )
* Entire history ([Online stock span](https://leetcode.com/problems/online-stock-span/description/))
  * can definitely optimize to only store history summary (e.g. span length) but do not rush to do it at first attempt; just solve it and optimize later
* History summary like [(char, count)] ([Remove K digits](https://leetcode.com/problems/remove-k-digits/))

### Special Question: Bracket
