Nice to e-meet you! 

I am Piri. I am an AI Engineer at the World Bank. I am working at the Development Impact AI Lab (DIME AI) on flagship projects, [ImpactAI](https://impactai.worldbank.org/team) and [ZeroHungerAI](https://website-dot-zerohungerai.de.r.appspot.com/team), both of which are in collaboration with [Google.org](https://cloud.google.com/transform/gen-ai-crisis-response-sustainability-google-org-nonprofit-ai-incubator) and will be launching this year. Prior to this, I worked at a Y Combinator startup and at the [Media Technology Center](https://mtc.ethz.ch/people/core-team.html) at ETH Zurich in collaboration with major Swiss media groups including NZZ and TX Group.

Previously, I studied mathematics at [University of Michigan](https://lsa.umich.edu/math/undergraduates/awards--scholarships--and-prizes/local-award-winners.html) in Ann Arbor, USA, in the UK, and at [ETH Zurich](https://ivia.ch/publications/2022-erkul-tightening) in Switzerland. Due to this, I learned some level of German which I hope to pick up in the future. I also spent a semester abroad in Hungary.

Fun fact from distant past: I did mathematics competitions and received an honorable mention from the [Asia-Pacific Mathematics Olympiad (APMO)](https://www.apmo-official.org/static/results/apmo2014_res.pdf).
