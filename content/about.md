Nice to e-meet you! 

I am Piri. I am an AI Engineer at the World Bank. I am working at the Development Impact AI Lab (DIME AI) on flagship projects, [ImpactAI](https://impactai.worldbank.org/team) and [ZeroHungerAI](https://website-dot-zerohungerai.de.r.appspot.com/team), both of which are launching this year. Prior to this, I worked at Typewise, a Y Combinator startup, and at the Media Technology Center at ETH Zurich in collaboration with major Swiss media groups including NZZ and TX Group.

Previously, I studied mathematics at University of Michigan in Ann Arbor and at University of Oxford in the UK, and then statistics at ETH Zurich in Switzerland. I also spent a semester abroad in the [Budapest Semesters in Mathematics program](https://budapestsemesters.com/) in Hungary.
